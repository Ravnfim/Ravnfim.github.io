01 — About

Who I am &
what this is

I'm Ravn Alnæs Fimland, 22 years old and currently completing a Bachelor's in Finance at BI Norwegian Business School. I have no formal industry experience yet — everything here comes from self-study, curiosity, and what I've built from my own initiative alongside coursework.

What draws me to investing is the intersection of psychology, mathematics, and genuine business analysis — the idea that every company is a unique case, a distinct attempt at creating something of value. Understanding one well means understanding its operations, its people, its competitive position. That breadth is what I find compelling.

My approach is bottom-up and opinionated. I try to think independently, weigh upside against downside carefully, and form views I can defend. The goal is to be right in a way that is asymmetric — win meaningfully when the thesis plays out, lose little when it doesn't.

This site documents that learning process. The analysis and opinions here are entirely my own and are not investment advice in any form. I am not a licensed investment advisor, and this content is for my personal learning and documentation only. Do your own research and consult qualified professionals before making investment decisions.
