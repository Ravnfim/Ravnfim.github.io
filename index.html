01 — About

Who I am &
what this is

I'm Ravn Alnæs Fimland, a finance student at BI Norwegian Business School. What draws me to investing is the intersection of psychology, mathematics, and genuine business analysis — the idea that every company is a unique case, a distinct attempt at creating something of value. Understanding one well means understanding its operations, its people, its competitive position. That breadth is what I find compelling.

My approach is bottom-up and opinionated. I try to think independently, weigh upside against downside carefully, and form views I can defend. The goal is to be right in a way that is asymmetric — win meaningfully when the thesis plays out, lose little when it doesn't. Current focus: technology and software.

This site exists for my own development and learning as much as anything else. The analysis and opinions published here are entirely my own and are not investment advice in any form. Do your own research.
